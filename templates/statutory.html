Anti-Ragging Committee

The committee ensures a ragging-free environment in the campus through proactive measures and prompt grievance redressal. Key functions include:

  • Regular campus inspections
  • 24/7 helpline for students
  • Awareness programs and workshops

Women’s Grievance Cell

Dedicated to addressing issues related to gender discrimination and ensuring a safe environment for female students. Key initiatives:

  • Confidential complaint handling
  • Self-defense training programs
  • Legal awareness workshops

Students’ Grievance Cell

Ensures prompt resolution of academic and administrative grievances through transparent processes. Features include:

  • Online complaint portal
  • Fortnightly review meetings
  • Student representation in committee
