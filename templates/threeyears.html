About Course

The 3-year LL.B. program is a standalone Bachelor’s Degree in Law, designed as per BCI regulations under the Advocates Act, 1961. This program enables graduates to enroll as Advocates upon successful completion.

Spanning three academic years, this course includes a six-month practical training component in the final semester, ensuring that students gain real-world legal experience. Additionally, the program emphasizes:

  • Case study-based learning.
  • Legal workshops with industry experts.
  • National, international, and interstate legal seminars.

Eligibility & Admission Criteria

  • Completion of 10+2 or equivalent qualification.
  • Minimum 50% marks in qualifying exams.
  • Entrance test and personal interview.
  • Strong analytical and communication skills.

Career Opportunities

  • Legal Practitioner (Lawyer, Advocate)
  • Judicial Services (Judge, Magistrate)
  • Corporate Legal Advisor
  • Legal Researcher and Academician

Fees and Funding

Scholarships and financial aid are available for meritorious students. The course fee covers tuition, library access, and internship programs.
