Meet Our Management

Ilahia Law College operates under the stewardship of Ilahia Trust, an organization committed to academic excellence and community development. Established in 1992, Ilahia Trust was founded by a visionary group from diverse backgrounds, unified by the mission of providing quality education to the region.

The Trust initially marked its presence with the establishment of Kavumkara Muslim L.P. School and Ilahia Public School, both in 1992. Over the years, it has expanded its educational footprint, including Ilahia College of Arts & Science and an esteemed Engineering College at Mulavoor.
